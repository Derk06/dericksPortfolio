Introduction

Hi, I’m Derick Luke a robotics enthusiast and aspiring engineer currently studying Engineering (Honours) in Mechatronics at the University of Wollongong.


I’ve always had a passion for building things, especially combining mechanics, electronics, and programming to create smart systems that solve real-world problems. Over the years, I’ve worked on a variety of projects from coding my first Python programs and designing motion-tracking systems to developing self-learning robots using reinforcement learning. I’m curious, self-driven, and always eager to explore new technologies and ideas in robotics, AI, and machine learning.
